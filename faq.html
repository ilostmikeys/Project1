FAQ

------------------------

  • Getting Started with GoShare?

    • Reviews are available to everyone in order to help them make better decisions as well as allowing them to gain a better insight in relation to which courses they may wish to take.
    • Help them prepare for the course even during the holidays before the semester starts.
    • Students are able to view courses based on peer reviews/opinions - can decide whether or not to take up the course.
    • Ability to upload, download written slide to be able to use notes instead of wasting it.

  • How to login?

    • Click on Log in from GoShare Welcome page
    • Enter your username & password
    • Click on Login

  • How to view your profile information?

    • Once you are login to your GoShare account then click on profile tab from menu bar.

  • How to upload a file?

    • Login to your account
    • Click on Document tab from menu bar
    • Click on Choose File button
    • Browse your file destination
    • Click on Submit

  • How to review a course?

    • Login to your account
    • Click on Course Information tab from menu bar
    • Search for course you would like to review
    • Browse the course name
    • Review the course
    • Click on Submit

  • How to rate a course?

    • Login to your account
    • Click on Course Information tab from menu bar
    • Search for course you would like to rate
    • Browse the course name
    • Rate the course by clicking the stars
